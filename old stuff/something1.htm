Maze Solver


with Jack Neus and Abiram Sritharam

Created at HackRU (Spring 2014)


Inspired by one of our Computer Science teachers at our school, we decided to create a Maze generator and solver with Java. Maze Solver has three key features.


1. Maze Generation


The user is first prompted for the height, width, and cell size, and a maze is then created using the Union Find algoithm. The Union Find algoithm generates a maze with all walls intact. The program randomly selects a cell and deletes one of the walls. This ensures that the maze is connected.


2. Human Solver (Yellow Line)


The Maze is first solved in the same manner as an intelligent human by keeping a "hand" on the left wall at all times.


3. Computer Solver (Red Line)


The maze is then solved in shortest distance possible.
